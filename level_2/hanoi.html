Owl pellet rules

  • Tap a root: pick up its top pellet, or drop what you hold.
  • Only one pellet per move.
  • Never place a bigger pellet on a smaller one.
  • Largest stays on the bottom of the stack.

Goal: build the full tower on the right root (warmer color). The middle root also counts if you finish there.
